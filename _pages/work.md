**Qualifications**
10th / 12th / II PUC

**Experience**
0-2 years

**Skills**
* Basic use of excel
* Ability to use a smartphone, change settings, install apps
* Ability to meet deadlines
* Ability to speak any two languages proficiently: kannada, hindi, english
